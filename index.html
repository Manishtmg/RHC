Welcome

Welcome to Redemption Hill Nepali Church! Located in the heart of our community, Redemption Hill is a place where faith, love, and cultural heritage come together. We are dedicated to spreading the message of hope and salvation through Jesus Christ, and we embrace the rich traditions and language of Nepal. Our church provides a welcoming environment for worship, fellowship, and spiritual growth. Whether you're a lifelong Christian or seeking to learn more about the faith, Redemption Hill Nepali Church invites you to join us in experiencing the transformative power of God's love.
